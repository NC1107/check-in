# Check-In Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 26, 2026**

Check-In is a private, invite-only social feed for close friends and family. It is
self-hosted and operated by the individual who runs your server ("we", "us"). This policy
explains what data the app handles and why. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use
third-party advertising or tracking.

## Information we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work:

- **Account information** — your name, phone number, and birthday, provided when you sign
  up. Your phone number is used to sign in and to match invitations.
- **Content you create** — the photos, text posts, comments, and reactions you share, plus
  an optional place label (e.g. "City, Country").
- **Approximate location (optional)** — if a photo you post contains GPS data, the app reads
  it **on your device** and converts it to a coarse "City, Country" label. The precise GPS
  coordinates never leave your device; only the label you choose to attach is stored.
- **Contacts (only when you invite someone)** — when you use the invite feature, the app
  lets you pick people from your phone's contacts. Only the phone numbers you explicitly
  select are sent to the server to create invitations. We do not upload your full address
  book.
- **Push notification token** — a device token used to deliver notifications about new
  activity, via Apple Push Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging.

## How we use your information

Your information is used solely to operate the app: to show your group's feed, deliver
notifications, manage invitations, and keep your account secure. We do **not** use it for
advertising, profiling, or analytics, and we do **not** sell or rent it to anyone.

## Who can see your content

Content you post is visible only to the members of your private server — the people who
were invited. It is not public and is not shared with outside parties. We use Apple Push
Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging only to deliver notification messages to
your devices.

## Data storage, security, and retention

Your data is stored on the self-hosted server that runs Check-In. Connections are encrypted
over HTTPS, and passwords are stored only as salted hashes (argon2id) — never in plain text.
We keep your data while your account is active. When your account is deleted, your account
information and the content you created are removed.

## Your choices and rights

- You can edit your profile and delete your own posts at any time in the app.
- You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, including deletion of
  your account, by contacting us at the email below.
- You can revoke the app's access to your photos, contacts, and notifications at any time in
  your device's iOS Settings.

## Children's privacy

Check-In is invite-only and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating
the effective date above.
